Self-Care

  • Do face masks
  • Get a mani-pedi
  • Get a massage
  • Enjoy your favorite food
  • Paint or draw

Family/Friend Time

  • Talk to loved ones
  • Connect with peers
  • Go out with friends
  • Plan a night in
  • Hug someone you love
  • Always stay in touch

Active Time

  • Yoga/Stretch
  • Swim
  • Go to the gym
  • Play with pets
  • Go on a run or walk
  • Try a sport

Outdoors Time

  • Hike
  • Go on a bike ride
  • Meditate
  • Go camping
  • Take a lunch break
  • Practice mindfulness

Home Time

  • De-clutter your space
  • Cook a new recipe
  • Watch a show/movie
  • Journal
  • Take a nap
  • Unplug electronics

  • Skip caffeine
  • Stick to a schedule
  • Make your bed cozy
  • Create a sanctuary
  • Exercise to increase sleep
  • Plan for the next day
